Automation isn't a buzzword on my projects — it's how I keep small teams from drowning in busywork. Here are five things I wire up again and again.

## 1. Lead routing on autopilot

Form submissions get parsed, tagged and pushed straight into a CRM or a Slack channel — with an instant, branded auto-reply to the visitor. No lead sits unread.

## 2. AI-assisted content drafts

I connect **Claude / ChatGPT** to a content pipeline so product descriptions, blog outlines and FAQs start as solid drafts, ready for a human polish.

## 3. Ops & reporting digests

**n8n** and **Zapier** flows collect the week's analytics, orders and uptime into one clean email — no dashboard-hopping.

## 4. Support triage

Incoming messages get classified and answered (or escalated) automatically, so common questions never wait for office hours.

## 5. Sync between the tools you already use

Sheets ↔ CRM ↔ email ↔ site — the glue that stops anyone copy-pasting data by hand.
